Summary: Very mixed reviews. Some students credit him with helping them finally pass calculus; others report excessive homework (300 problems/week), exams disconnected from course material, and poor explanations. Results seem highly dependent on individual learning style. Students who need hand-holding or clear explanations should consider alternatives.
