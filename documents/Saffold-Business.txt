Review 1:
One of the best professors at UWM. He actually made the lectures interesting and has a funny sense of humor. He also makes his tests similar to the lecture slides. I would recommend him. He has a harsh sense of humor sometimes but he's an amazing teacher.

Review 2:
Best professor ever. Pushes engagement, doesn't favor anyone, never made deals about finals. Participation helps your grade, openly admitted this. 3 exams, HW each chapter, SI reviews. People who dislike him just don't do the work, and those who think it's hard never showed up. Exams are around 55-60 questions with 5-10 freebies, so 50/55 can still be 100%. Participation matters.

Review 3:
I had him last semester and it was one of my favorite classes at UWM. You actually learn things that will be helpful throughout life. He doesn't like disengaged students but his lectures are fun so paying attention isn't hard!

Review 4:
Decent professor. Great in person, but bad online content. His lectures were pretty good — we would participate in large conversations with him regarding each subject, where he would encourage us to question the law as much as possible, which helped me learn.

Review 5:
Didn't open the class until 2-3 days after classes started and immediately had a 0 because the professor used an old premade canvas course with data already in it. It's an easy class if you're taking it online but I wouldn't recommend it. Rude at times.
