Review 1:
I had Phil for Math 231 and 232. He spent the entirety of the 2-hour lecture (other than a short break) teaching what you NEED. He gives great examples and can answer any of your questions. He is the best math teacher I have ever had and would recommend him to everyone. UTILIZE HIS OFFICE HOURS if you need help — he is great to be around.

Review 2:
I had to take Calc I three times, and Phil helped me get a B on the 3rd go around. His lectures always left people laughing from small talk, he allowed group discussion when solving problems, and didn't give out enormous amounts of homework. He wants you to learn and succeed. Would highly recommend. He does Calc I and Calc II (mainly the latter).

Review 3:
One of the best math professors I've had. He made almost 2-hour class lectures easy to follow and was great at giving feedback. Phil wants you to learn.

Review 4:
He always dedicated his classes to be sure we weren't just able to pass an exam, but that we knew what we were doing. I am someone who has always struggled with math, but in his class I wasn't just doing math — I was learning how to apply it.

Summary: Consistently excellent reviews. Teaches Math 231 (Calc I) and 232 (Calc II), mainly Calc II. 2-hour lectures that are engaging with small talk and group discussion. Minimal but focused homework. Students who struggled with calculus elsewhere often succeed with Schmidt. Strongly recommended for anyone taking Calc I or II at UWM. Office hours are highly productive.
